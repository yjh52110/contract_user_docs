CherryIN
========

1.  点击 CherryIN 服务商的 "点击这里获取密钥"
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-1994a02f3e8d159e5f6514b302ed56472a14bec2%252Fimage%2520%28156%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=de528137&sv=2)

1.  在 CherryIN 的控制台中创建密钥，注意创建密钥时，根据令牌分组不同，模型倍率不同，即折扣不同。
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-35e719f3c7ba557db9d40e54264e35ef1ab879c5%252Fimage%2520%28157%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=cc9ee069&sv=2)

1.  点击密钥后方的按钮，复制密钥到剪贴板
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-3780989917fd1429959eec7d305b7c89ea5fe764%252Fimage%2520%28159%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=7d5aa781&sv=2)

1.  在 Cherry Studio 中填入密钥
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-9427649cce9d296fcdb2be09479788e5115967bf%252Fimage%2520%28160%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=45451b56&sv=2)

1.  点击管理按钮，并添加模型
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-6c6fff9e018cd25312c80de790dcda0d9d54a5f9%252Fimage%2520%28162%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=42cd3a60&sv=2)

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-77fb3ee8c8d7549303e096600361096c24e597fd%252Fimage%2520%28163%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=a89872ab&sv=2)
