Google Gemini
=============

[hashtag](#huo-qu-apikey)

获取APIKey

---------------------------------------

*   获取 Gemini 的 api key 前，你需要有一个 Google Cloud 项目（如果你已有，此过程可跳过）
    
*   进入 [Google Cloud](https://console.cloud.google.com/projectcreate) 创建项目，填写项目名称并点击创建项目
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-1b672d0d3a7c91ec2029c67bf61bd841e60ab506%252Fimage%2520%2874%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=7a2d7cdd&sv=2)

*   在官方 [API Key页面](https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey?hl=zh-cn) 点击 `密钥 创建API密钥`
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-d25a68f843733f29ba0b541b64499296a8eec5ad%252Fimage%2520%2872%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=b6d127e2&sv=2)

*   将生成的 key 复制，并打开 CherryStudio 的 [服务商设置](https://github.com/CherryHQ/cherry-studio-docs/blob/main/pre-basic/providers/broken-reference/README.md)
    
*   找到服务商 Gemini，填入刚刚获取到的 key
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-aac8dc1f791d2a5acdcb9c9892e5bd9b69e34b99%252Fimage%2520%2875%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=6b4386fb&sv=2)

*   点击最下方管理或者添加，加入支持的模型并打开右上角服务商开关就可以使用了。
    

circle-info

*   中国除台湾之外其他地区无法直接使用 Google Gemini 服务，需自行解决代理问题；
