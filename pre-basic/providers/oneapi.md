*   创建新令牌（也可以直接使用default令牌↑）
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-8f9da4239594f17807bff1c5cb62e8a817e338cf%252Fimage%2520%2819%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=bfb0e29&sv=2)

*   复制令牌
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-ed0d9ce1b6642b8d9e23bb2aaee4e4db9369096e%252Fimage%2520%2823%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=69210f85&sv=2)

*   打开CherryStudio的服务商设置点击服务商列表最下方的`添加`
    
*   输入备注名称，提供商选OpenAI，点击确定
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-576769f8965a56f182925e3209690d9459a73e26%252Fimage%2520%2825%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=6f3f5485&sv=2)

*   填入刚刚复制的key
    
*   回到获取API Key的页面，在对应浏览器地址栏复制根地址，例：
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-91a2b54ef14a03e7453c00305e938e84ce93684b%252Fimage%2520%2826%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=a5efd937&sv=2)

**只需要复制https://xxx.xxx.com即可，“/”及其之后的内容不需要**

circle-info

*   当地址为IP+端口时填http://IP:端口即可，如：http://127.0.0.1:3000
    
*   严格区分`http`和`https`，如果没有开启SSL就不要填https
    

*   添加模型（点击管理自动获取或手动输入）打开右上角开关即可使用。
    

circle-check

OneAPI其他主题可能界面有所不同，但添加方法跟上述操作流程一致。
