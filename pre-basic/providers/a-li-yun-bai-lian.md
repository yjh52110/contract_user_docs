阿里云百炼
=====

1.  登录 [阿里云百炼](https://bailian.console.aliyun.com/?tab=model#/api-key)，没有阿里云账号的话需要注册。
    
2.  点击右上角的 `创建我的 API-KEY` 按钮。
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-3afabb5cfda8aaf4bb4bb480d7265e7b280c37a9%252F%25E5%2588%259B%25E5%25BB%25BAAPI%25E5%25AF%2586%25E9%2592%25A5.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=269d1c78&sv=2)

阿里云百炼创建API密钥

1.  在弹出的窗口中选择默认业务空间（或者你也可以自定义），如果你想要的话可以填入描述。
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-861e842abd3ebc6c97cc3ba789fe38c0e7ed54d1%252F%25E5%2588%259B%25E5%25BB%25BAAPI%25E5%25AF%2586%25E9%2592%25A5%25E5%25BC%25B9%25E7%25AA%2597.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=ab4d842c&sv=2)

阿里云百炼创建API密钥弹窗

1.  点击右下角的 `确定` 按钮。
    
2.  随后，你应该能看到列表中新增了一行，点击右侧的 `查看` 按钮。
    
    ![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-6273a9fb54c68167699b2c7412263a2e1a489770%252F%25E6%259F%25A5%25E7%259C%258BAPI%25E5%25AF%2586%25E9%2592%25A5.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=161e1508&sv=2)
    
    阿里云百炼查看API密钥
    
3.  点击 `复制` 按钮。
    
    ![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-3177eae4b7c88eb96f881e1c3fcb04dc6f884fe2%252F%25E5%25A4%258D%25E5%2588%25B6API%25E5%25AF%2586%25E9%2592%25A5.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=ce163eb5&sv=2)
    
    阿里云百炼复制API密钥
    
4.  转到 Cherry Studio，在 `设置` → `模型服务` → `阿里云百炼` 中找到 `API 密钥` ，将复制的 API 密钥粘贴到这里。
    
    ![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-3c782f815651a77ef8320a81250b8eb2c0b5781c%252F%25E5%25A1%25AB%25E5%2585%25A5API%25E5%25AF%2586%25E9%2592%25A5.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=6413f4f2&sv=2)
    
    阿里云百炼填入API密钥
    
5.  可以按照 [模型服务](/pre-basic/settings/providers) 中的介绍调整相关设置，然后就能使用了。
    

circle-info

如果发现模型列表中没有阿里云百炼的模型，请确认已经按照 [模型服务](/pre-basic/settings/providers) 中的介绍添加模型，并开启了这个提供商。
