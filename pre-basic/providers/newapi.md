NewAPI
======

*   登录并打开令牌页面
    
*   点击添加令牌
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-1eb29a3442586cb00f74698d90f5da53c4c989f1%252Fimage%2520%2828%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=a1294385&sv=2)

*   输入令牌名称后点击提交（其他设置如有需要可自行配置）
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-6bf089e6345ec76964053d8b6b58d5b88a86ac7d%252Fimage%2520%2829%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=2b72ec27&sv=2)

*   打开CherryStudio的服务商设置点击服务商列表最下方的`添加`
    
*   输入备注名称，提供商选OpenAI，点击确定
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-576769f8965a56f182925e3209690d9459a73e26%252Fimage%2520%2825%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=6f3f5485&sv=2)

*   填入刚刚复制的key
    
*   回到获取API Key的页面，在对应浏览器地址栏复制根地址，例：
    

![](https://docs.cherry-ai.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3562065924-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F0Ut5BptC3t8CtSU1UWpM%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-56da5ef2284935a4ae3fef288fd624d108dca0c9%252Fimage%2520%2830%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=5aa4b4c&sv=2)

**只需要复制https://xxx.xxx.com即可，“/”及其之后的内容不需要**

circle-info

*   当地址为IP+端口时填http://IP:端口即可，如：http://127.0.0.1:3000
    
*   严格区分`http`和`https`，如果没有开启SSL就不要填https
    

*   添加模型（点击管理自动获取或手动输入）打开右上角开关即可使用。
