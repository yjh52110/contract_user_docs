### Step 2: Enter a Contract Address

1. Paste the contract address (starts with 0x) into the search box
2. Select the blockchain network
3. Click **"Start Audit"**

### Step 3: Review the Audit Report

After the audit completes, you will see a full security report, including:

- **Security Score**: 0-100 score, higher is safer
- **Vulnerability List**: Issues ranked by severity
- **Fix Suggestions**: Recommended remediation steps
